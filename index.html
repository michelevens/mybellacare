Get to Know Us

About Clearstone Group

Clearstone Group, LLC is a Florida-based healthcare services company dedicated to empowering individuals with disabilities, seniors, and families through compassionate, person-centered care. From residential habilitation to private duty nursing, every service we provide is built on the foundation of dignity, respect, and genuine human connection.

As an approved APD (Agency for Persons with Disabilities) provider, we specialize in serving individuals with developmental and intellectual disabilities — helping them live more independent, fulfilling lives within their communities. Our team of experienced Direct Support Professionals, Home Health Aides, and nursing staff are the heart of everything we do.

Healthcare Staffing Solutions

Partnering with facilities to solve staffing shortages with qualified, vetted healthcare professionals.

The Problem

Healthcare facilities across Florida face chronic staffing shortages. Unfilled shifts mean compromised care, overworked teams, and regulatory risk.

Our Solution

Clearstone Group provides fully vetted, background-checked healthcare professionals — DSPs, HHAs, CNAs, Med Techs, and Caregivers — on flexible schedules to keep your facility fully staffed.

How It Works

  1. Tell us your staffing needs and schedule
  2. We match qualified professionals to your facility
  3. Our staff arrive trained, credentialed, and ready
  4. Ongoing quality assurance and support
