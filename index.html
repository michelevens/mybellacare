What We Offer

Our Services

Tailored residential and in-home care solutions to meet the unique needs of every individual and family.

🏠

Assisted Living Facility

A safe, supportive home environment for seniors who need help with daily activities while maintaining their independence and quality of life.

  • Medication management
  • Meal preparation & nutrition
  • Personal hygiene assistance
  • Social activities & recreation
  • 24/7 staff supervision
🏡

Group Home

Small, intimate residential settings providing personalized care for individuals with developmental disabilities, mental health needs, or aging-related conditions.

  • Individualized care plans
  • Community integration support
  • Life skills development
  • Behavioral health support
  • Family-like atmosphere
🤝

Personal Support

Companionship and daily living assistance that helps individuals stay comfortable, connected, and thriving in their own homes.

  • Companionship & conversation
  • Light housekeeping
  • Errands & transportation
  • Meal planning & preparation
  • Appointment coordination
☤

Private Duty Nursing

One-on-one skilled nursing care delivered in the comfort of home — for patients who need clinical attention without the hospital setting.

  • Registered Nurse (RN) care
  • Wound care & IV therapy
  • Post-surgical recovery
  • Chronic disease management
  • Pediatric & adult nursing
Who We Are

About BellaCare

BellaCare was founded on a simple belief: every person deserves compassionate, high-quality care in a place that feels like home. We are a Florida-based residential healthcare provider committed to enriching the lives of those we serve.

Our team of licensed professionals — nurses, caregivers, and support staff — brings years of experience in assisted living, group home management, and private duty nursing. We treat every resident and client like family.
